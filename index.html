About Me

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Computer Science in Multi-Agent Artificial Intelligence.
My research involves developing scalable algorithms to study communication and interaction among
autonomous agents in partially observable and stochastic environment.
My research interests are Decision Theoretic planning, Reinforcement Learning, and Multi-Agent Systems
